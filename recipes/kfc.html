Korean Fried Chicken

Home

Description

Korean fried chicken is an iconic must-have dish at drinking parties with friends. The difference between Korean fried chicken and regular, American fried chicken is the sweet, spicy, irresistable sauce that coats the fried shell. This recipe uses potato starch instead of regular flour to get that extra crispy exterior. After following these simple and easy steps, you'll have a delicious dish that'll bring the party to another level!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chicken legs
  • 2 cups potato starch
  • salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar

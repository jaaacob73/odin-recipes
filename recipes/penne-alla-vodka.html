Penne Alla Vodka

Description

This penne alla vodka will delight you with perfectly-cooked penne pasta blanketed in a light, creamy vodka sauce. All you need to do is follow these simple instructions and you'll end up with a delicious and easy dish perfect for any occasion, whether you're trying to impress a significant other or you're in a rush for work. And here's the best part: it takes less than half an hour to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 can or bottle of tomato or marinara sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup vodka
  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons parsley
  • 3 basil leaves
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Get all ingredients out and prepare them appropriately
  2. Get a pot of water on high heat and generously salt the water
  3. When water boils, put in pasta and let it cook for 11-13 minutes
  4. Get a pan on medium heat and
  5. Once olive oil is heated, add minced garlic and cook until they're slightly colored
  6. Add in tomato sauce, heavy cream, basil, and parmesan cheese and stir for 5-7 minutes
  7. Once pasta is cooked, add it to sauce and mix until all the pasta is coated
  8. Adjust tasta with salt and pepper if needed
  9. Add it to a bowl or plate and top off with parsley and a little more parmesan cheese
  10. Enjoy!

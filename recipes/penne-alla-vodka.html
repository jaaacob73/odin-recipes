Description

This penne alla vodka will delight you with perfectly-cooked penne pasta blanketed in a light, creamy vodka sauce. All you need to do is follow these simple instructions and you'll end up with a delicious and easy dish perfect for any occasion, whether you're trying to impress a significant other or you're in a rush for work. And here's the best part: it takes less than half an hour to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 can or bottle of tomato or marinara sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup vodka
  • 3 slices of bacon (or more, according to preference)
  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 2/3 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons parsley
  • 3 basil leaves
  • salt and pepper to taste
